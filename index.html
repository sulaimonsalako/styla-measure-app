💡 Method 1: Safari Bookmark (Easiest for iPad)

No App Needed

You don't need to touch the Shortcuts app at all! Just save STYLA as a Safari bookmark on your iPad:

  1. Tap Copy Bookmark Code below.
  2. In Safari on your iPad, tap Share ↗️ → Add Bookmark.
  3. Edit the bookmark, rename it ⚡ STYLA Stylist, and paste the code into the Address field.
  4. When shopping on any site (RW&CO, Zara, etc.), just tap your Bookmarks menu and tap STYLA!
