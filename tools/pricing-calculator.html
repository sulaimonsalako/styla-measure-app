Brand Sourcing & Pricing Suite

Solve factory MOQ cost barriers and project profit margins using our interactive tier tools.

💡 Pricing Calculator Guide (Click to Expand)

Launching a clothing brand requires navigating manufacturer pricing variables. This suite offers two distinct apps to analyze your pricing strategy:

1. Solve Price from Target Margin %

Enter the production quotes you got from your factory and shipping rates to find what retail MSRP you must charge to maintain a target profit margin %.

2. Solve Price from Flat Order Profit ($)

Set a target **flat profit wage** for your time per batch (e.g. $500). Compare how higher order sizes drop your required customer selling price, allowing you to price competitively.

3. Click the (?) Help Icons

Every pricing field (Sample Fee, Packaging, Customs, MOQ) has a ? icon. Click them to view exact math equations and descriptions of how that cost impacts your margin.
