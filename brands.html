How STYLA Solves the Sizing Pain

📱

1. Dynamic Customer Sizing Links

Generate custom invitation links that you can email, text (SMS), or send via order confirmations directly to your customers. Customers run a quick, secure 3D scan on their mobile devices in seconds.

  • Collect aggregated, anonymized size demographics
  • Identify sizing gaps and voids in your catalogs
  • Optimize future production size distribution splits
  • Avoid overstocking or stockouts of key sizes
✂

2. On-Site Fit Recommendation

STYLA tracks and securely stores consumer measurements. When they visit your e-commerce website, our widget compares their actual body measurements to your garments' size charts.

  • Instant, personalized size recommendation
  • Compares measurements down to chest, waist, hips, and inseam
  • Eliminates "what size is the model wearing?" queries
  • Prevents bracket shopping (buying 2 sizes to return 1)
🤖

3. AI-Powered Product Widget

Go beyond static tables. Our sizing widget integrates an AI assistant trained on product metrics, material stretch, shrinkage, and customer reviews to answer shopper queries instantly.

  • Answers detailed queries: "Is this tight around the chest?"
  • Understands material properties: fabric blend, elasticity, cut
  • Generates expert answers in milliseconds
  • Slashes buyer friction, lifting checkouts instantly
🌱

Improve Your Environmental & Waste Score

Returns aren't just expensive for your margins—they are catastrophic for the environment. Sizing returns double logistics emissions and contribute millions of tons of textile waste to landfills. By matching customers with their correct size before they buy, STYLA helps your brand drastically improve its environmental rating, promoting circularity and zero-waste retail values.
