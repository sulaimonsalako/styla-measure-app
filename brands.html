How We Help Brands

Sizing Confidence. Simplified.

📱

1. Aggregate Customer Data

We generate SMS/Email scan links you can send directly to your customer base. Shoppers perform a quick mobile 3D body scan, and you receive aggregated, anonymous size demographics to produce better fitting garments, select correct size distributions, and meet unfilled sizing demand.

✂

2. On-Site Fit Recommendations

We securely collect customer body measurements and instantly find their perfect size when they browse your website. Our system automatically matches their exact body proportions against your size chart or garment chart so they shop with absolute fit confidence.

🤖

3. AI Sizing & product Widget

Embed our sizing widget trained on your specific garments, fabrics, stretch, and customer feedback. Shoppers get instant, expert answers to sizing and product fit questions in real-time, removing friction and boosting conversions.

🌱

Improve Your Environmental Rating

Sizing returns double logistics emissions and contribute to massive fabric waste. By helping customers place orders in their correct size and aligning your manufacturing cuts to real-world dimensions, STYLA significantly improves your brand's environmental score.
