# Styla Measure Marketing Hooks Library

This library contains **10 core marketing hooks** tailored for the key features of the Styla Measure ecosystem:
1. **Styla Measure** (AI body scanning & automated size decoding)
2. **MOQ Calculator** (Pricing/cost analysis & manufacturing unit economics)
3. **Online Shopping** (Sizing anxiety & return cycles)
4. **Shopping from African Brands** (Bespoke custom tailoring, global sizing mismatch, and overseas returns)

## Implementation Examples (High-Emotion Post Outlines)

Here is how to structure posts using these hooks.

### Post Example A: MOQ Calculator (Hook #2)
*   **Hook**: The uncomfortable truth about bulk discounts nobody tells you.
*   **Problem**: You order 1,000 units instead of 200 because the supplier gave you a 40% discount per unit. Now, $8,000 of your working capital is gathering dust in a garage. You are cash-poor and inventory-rich.
*   **Solution**: Smart brand owners use the STYLA MOQ Calculator. It factors in freight, storage, and sales velocity to show you the *true* break-even MOQ. Sometimes, paying 20% more per unit on a smaller order is the most profitable decision you can make.
*   **CTA**: Stop tying up your cash. Use our free MOQ & unit economics calculator to find your profit sweet spot. Link in bio.

### Post Example B: Shopping African Brands (Hook #3)
*   **Hook**: You don't actually need to see a local tailor in person to fix sizing errors on your overseas custom apparel orders.
*   **Problem**: You fall in love with an authentic Ankara piece from a Nigerian designer, but you're terrified of sending the wrong measurements. You know a return back to Africa will cost more than the dress itself. You hesitate and miss out on stunning, unique fashion.
*   **Solution**: STYLA Measure decodes the gap between local tailoring and global buyers. You take a quick body scan, and we generate an exact 3D spec sheet you can send directly to any designer. They get precise tailoring measurements, you get a custom fit that flatters your exact shape.
*   **CTA**: Shop global, fit local. Get your digital body spec sheet with STYLA today. Link in bio.
